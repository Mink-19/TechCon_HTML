Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the past 14 years, it has grown into one of the world's premier tech conferences, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. We bring together innovators, developers, and business leaders to shape the future of technology.

Our core principles:

  • Fostering innovation through collaboration
  • Making technology accessible to all
  • Promoting ethical tech development
